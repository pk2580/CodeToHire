Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service for students from non-CS/IT branches?

Absolutely! We help students from all engineering branches who want to get a job in the IT sector. We will create a custom plan to help you build the necessary skills.

Do you guarantee a job?

We do not guarantee a job, as that ultimately depends on your performance. However, we guarantee to provide you with the best possible preparation, resources, and strategy to significantly boost your chances of getting hired.

What if I am a complete beginner in coding?

That's perfectly fine. Our initial consultation helps us understand your current level. We'll provide a roadmap starting from the very basics to get you interview-ready.

How are the mock interviews conducted?

Mock interviews are conducted online via Google Meet or Zoom. They are 1-on-1, live sessions designed to be as realistic as possible.
